Golden comets are know for high egg-production. Golden comets are a modern day egg-laying day strain of chicken. They are a cross between The White Plymouth Rock hen and Rhode Island Red rooster. Like leghorns, golden comets are know to lay at a very early age, some begin as early as 16 weeks.
